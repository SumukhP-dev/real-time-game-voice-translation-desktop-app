# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: December 2024

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Real-Time Game Voice Translation ("we", "our", or "the Software") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our software.

## Information We Collect

### License Information

- **License Key**: Stored locally on your computer in encrypted format
- **Hardware Fingerprint**: Used to bind your license to your computer (CPU ID, system information)
- **Activation Status**: Stored locally to verify license validity

### Usage Data

- **Application Logs**: Local logging for troubleshooting (stored on your computer only)
- **Error Reports**: May be collected if you choose to report errors (optional)

### Translation Data

- **Audio Data**: Audio is processed locally on your computer
- **Transcription Text**: Speech-to-text conversion happens locally
- **Translation Requests**: Sent to Google Translate API (subject to Google's privacy policy)
- **No Storage**: We do not store, log, or transmit your audio or translation data

## How We Use Information

### License Validation

- Hardware fingerprint is used to validate your license key
- License information is stored locally in encrypted format
- No license data is transmitted to our servers (offline validation)

### Translation Services

- Translation requests are sent directly to Google Translate API
- We do not intercept, store, or log translation data
- Your translations are subject to Google's privacy policy

### Support and Troubleshooting

- If you contact support, we may ask for system information to help troubleshoot
- Error logs (if provided) are used only for debugging purposes
- No personal data is collected without your explicit consent

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

- License file: `%USERPROFILE%\.csgo2_translation\license.dat` (encrypted)
- Configuration: `config.json` (local settings)
- Audio recordings: `audio/` folder (optional, for debugging only)

### No Cloud Storage

- We do not store your data on remote servers
- All data remains on your local computer
- License validation works offline

## Third-Party Services

### Google Translate API

- Translation requests are sent to Google Translate
- Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- We do not control how Google handles translation data

### OpenAI Whisper

- Speech recognition uses OpenAI Whisper (local processing)
- Audio is processed on your computer
- No data is sent to OpenAI servers

## Data Security

- License files are encrypted using cryptographic methods
- Hardware fingerprinting uses standard system information
- No sensitive data is transmitted over the network (except translation API calls)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View your license information in the application
- **Deletion**: Delete license files to deactivate (contact support for license transfer)
- **Privacy**: All processing happens locally on your computer

## Children's Privacy

This Software is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
